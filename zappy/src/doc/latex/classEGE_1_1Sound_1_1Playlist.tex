\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a playlist of music tracks. 

The \doxylink{classEGE_1_1Sound_1_1Playlist}{Playlist} class allows you to manage a collection of music tracks and perform operations such as playing, pausing, stopping, and navigating between tracks.
